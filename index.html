Free prototype

We will build a prototype for you. Free of cost. Continue to build further only if you like it. We do so because we are confident in our ability and your app idea!.

Export

If have an internal team or a group of developers you work with, you can. Export the prototype and we will ship the code and data, ready to further developed on.

Portability

Apart from being able to export in developer friendly, we also support iOS apps, Android apps and chrome apps out of the box. You can also request export in a framework of your choice.

You own the application logic and data and can export it to develop further on it, or just host it on your own. Our process is built around that assumption and provides you complete flexibility.
